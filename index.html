NATIONAL DNA DAY
April 25th

 

DIY Genetic Testing Private genetic testing services like 23andMe, FamilyTree, Ancestry, MyHeritage and public like Jetmatch, Dna Land promise only to help you learn about yourself through your DNA. The popularity and network effect of DNA tests raise privacy questions: Do these companies own your DNA data? Can the state or private companies use this data against you? Are these open databases a de facto National DNA database?

 

Join us and Celebrate DNA Day by helping make Synthetic biology exciting, accessible and democratic to students and non-scientists citizens. All power levels welcome! No science background needed to take part in our workshop, most are new even to biology and bioengineering. Our Workshop intends to review and teach advanced BioTech in an easy-to-understand, hands-on way with DIY lab instruments. Our workshop will cover introductory biological concepts, basic lab techniques, genomics, synthetic biology, bioart and biodesign, and social implications of emerging biotech industry.
